Good to Know

A few helpful details to make your day smooth and joyful

What should I wear?

Traditional Indian wedding attire is encouraged. Ladies can wear sarees, lehengas, or anarkalis. Gentlemen can wear kurta pajamas, sherwanis, or suits. Please feel free to embrace vibrant colors and celebrate with us!

What time should I arrive?

For Day 1 (February 10), please check into your hotel by 1:00 PM and arrive at the venue by 5:00 PM for the Haldi ceremony. For Day 2 (February 11), please be present by 6:30 AM for the morning ceremonies.
